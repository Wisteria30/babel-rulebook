## Game Overview

4 players **cooperate** to instruct AI and build an **app (the tower)**.

### God (System) Interference

God won't allow the tower to be completed.  
Randomly interferes with **"Silence"** or **"Word Reversal"**.

### Hidden Traitor

Among the players hides a **God's Apostle**.

## Game Flow

Victory is determined after **4 rounds (4 days)**.


### Round Structure

Each round consists of 6 phases:

1. **Role Action** - Role-specific abilities
2. **Divine Judgment** - God's interference check
3. **Discussion** - Free discussion (~4 min)
4. **Exile** - Vote to suspend one player
5. **Implement** - Give AI instructions
6. **Check** - Verify tower status
